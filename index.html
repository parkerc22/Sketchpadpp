Press L/C to initiate a line/circle from cursor position. Click to finish it. A to zoom in S to zoom out. While hovering over a point, press M to move it. While hovering over a line, press E to initiate an equal length constraint and then click on the other line you would like to constrain. Use the P key to do the same for parallel line constraints. While hovering over any element, press D to delete it. While hovering over a line, press H to enforce it to be horizontal or V to enforce it to be vertical. Press T to tick constraint enforcement.
